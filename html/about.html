About me

Cooking has always been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. Growing up in Thailand, I always found myself in the kitchen, doing whatever I could to help prepare family dinners.

Thai recipes are everywhere on the internet, but I want to share something more authentic; true understanding of the steps and the ingredients, and even the cultural background of the dish. This is what really excites me, and this is what Asian Cooking is all about. All my recipes have been tested multiple times so you never have to worry if it'll work!
